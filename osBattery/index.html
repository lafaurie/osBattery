  • Tips
    Charge the battery whenever you can
    info
    Contrary to what many think, leave download the Li-Ion batteries completely, ie 0%, has a negative effect on them.
    info
    Fully recharge necessitates the use of a larger number of charging cycles.
    info
    It would be advisable to let them offload up to 40% or 50% capacity and recharged again.
    Do not charge the batteries to full capacity
    info
    Incredibly, it is recommended to charge the batteries in our devices up to 100% of its capacity, in fact it is advisable to load up to 80% or 90% of its total capacity.
    info
    In case you can not avoid loading up to 100%, ideally disconnect the charger when the limit is reached.
    info
    To stay connected more than necessary occurs in the battery a "mini-cycles" end load decreasing its life.
    Download full battery once a month
    info
    While it is a contradiction to the first point, there is a reason for this. The lithium ion batteries are also known to be intelligent, this "intelligence" is the device that allows us know about the approximate charging time remaining for the battery.
    info
    As a result of various processes of loading and unloading, these values are no longer accurate, to recalibrate the battery manufacturers recommend performing a full download.
    Use the correct charger for your battery type
    info
    Charging batteries using any charger helps reduce not only their life, but can also be dangerous.
    info
    A battery that connects to an inappropriate charger may overheat and even catch fire.
    info
    Try to always use the charger supplied with the battery itself or device that uses it.
    Do not worry about the first charge
    info
    Many times when buying a new device some vendors recommend that you fully charge the battery before first use, something for nothing, is necessary.
    info
    Operating a Li-Ion battery on the first charge is equal to any of the following.
    Store batteries in a cool place
    info
    In case you need to store a battery for a long time, it is best to leave it loaded up to 40% of its total capacity and put it to a temperature close to 15 Â° C.
    info
    The heat can damage the battery completely if left stored for long.
    info
    A lithium ion battery stored properly can retain up to 80% of their charge and work perfectly even after being unused for six months.
